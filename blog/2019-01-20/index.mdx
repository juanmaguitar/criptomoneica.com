## Delegation

Delegated proof of stake

> In PoS, **a security deposit is required to participate in the consensus process**. As in proof-of-work, the consensus protocol relies on an honest majority for its security which is incentivized directly by the Tezos protocol by penalizing dishonest behavior and rewarding honest behavior. If a participant behaves dishonestly, they can lose their deposit. Users who do not wish to participate directly in the consensus protocol **have the option to delegate their rights to other users to participate on their behalf**.
